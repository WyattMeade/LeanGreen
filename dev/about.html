A Little History

The tale behind this venture started out tragic and, ultimately, ended in sweet redemption. Our fearless founder, Brian Lucas, used to be fat. Like okra fat. He was stuck in a corporate job that offered long, thankless hours with zero regard for personal nutrition. On several occasions when Brian was tirelessly working late at his desk, he was forced to grab dinner from a depressing vending machine in the office break room. His choices were always the same. Cheetos. Zagnut Candy Bars. A pack of Lifesavers. These options were far from life saving. In fact, over the many years on this job Brian poured countless coins in this machine and only received a higher cholesterol level and protruding belly in return.

He finally decided he was ready for something different; both in a professional and personal respect. Having an innate entrepreneurial sense, Brian decided to

look into why vending machines, which were so often stocked with the same types of products, did not offer any healthy alternatives. He discovered no satisfying answers and immediately sensed an opportunity. That's when he bought a machine, filled it with luna bars and started talking to schools, offices and gyms about how things could be different. The response was resounding. People want to eat healthier and want more control over their diet.

One machine has now turned into a small army of machines in the Chicago-land area. Brian continues to scour the area, ridding environments of their once sad vending options and putting new life into people's daily snack choices. And, as for Brian, his gut is now gone and he lives by the namesake of his company, Leangreen Healthy Vending.

to make healthier snacks & drinks much more available to people throughout Chicago.

More About Us

Everyone loves snack time. A quick break from the routine of the day. Something salty, something sweet. But not everyone loves the serving of guilt that comes with typical snacks from traditional machines. These days, people are searching for more control and more options. At LeanGreen Vending, it's all about choice - healthier or comforting, unexpected or familiar, flavor explosion or simply nutty. We specialize in all-natural, more nutritious snacks & drinks, but we understand the need for a range of choices, which is why we work closely with all our clients to give the control back to the people. Snack time revolution has begun, Chicago!
